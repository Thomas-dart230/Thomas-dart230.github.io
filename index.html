Tom's Poem

By
Thomas Rourke

This is the story of someone great

Someone who will meet their tragic fate

No one knows the exact date

This happened at the time of eight

There was a waiter named Tate

Who loved to carry more than his weight

He was always very agile and classy when carrying his plate

Untile he dropped his plate and receieved so much hate

This ruined his estate

the greatness of Tate and his waiting skills are still up for
debate
